about

Every year about eight million animals will find their way to a shelter; almost three million of them will be euthanized. source

Mission

We believe every animal deserves a safe home and loving family. Our mission is to shelter the lost and abandoned and transfer animals from high-risk kill shelters across the nation to our no-kill facility.

Vision

We want to expand to multiple cities across the United States and reduce the percentage of euthanized shelter animals to a low, reasonable amount.

Goals

  • Find homes for 10,000 animals in 2016.
  • Expand to five nearby states by fall 2018
  • Reduce euthanization by 15% by 2020.
